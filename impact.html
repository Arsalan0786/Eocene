WASTE MANAGEMENT IMPACT

Why Waste Management Matters

Effective waste management is crucial for maintaining environmental balance and sustainability. Without proper disposal and recycling, waste can contribute to pollution, climate change, and resource depletion.

Environmental Impact

Poor waste management leads to air, soil, and water pollution. Landfills release methane, a greenhouse gas more potent than carbon dioxide. Proper waste segregation and recycling help reduce this impact.

Social & Economic Benefits

Efficient waste management creates jobs in recycling and waste processing industries, promotes a cleaner environment, and reduces public health risks caused by waste-related diseases.

Future Innovations in Waste Management

AI and smart waste management systems are revolutionizing waste collection, sorting, and recycling, making processes more efficient and sustainable.
