ABOUT WASTE MANAGEMENT

What is Waste Management?

Waste management refers to the processes involved in handling waste, from its collection to disposal. It includes practices such as recycling, composting, landfilling, and incineration.

Importance of Waste Management

Proper waste management helps in reducing pollution, conserving natural resources, and mitigating climate change. It ensures sustainable living for future generations.

Role of Technology in Waste Management

Technologies like AI, IoT, and smart waste collection systems are making waste management more efficient by optimizing resource allocation and waste sorting.
